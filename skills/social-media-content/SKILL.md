# Social Media Content

## Purpose

Create social content that fits the audience, platform, message goal, and brand voice while avoiding false urgency, fake authority, or engagement bait.

## Use When

Use for:

- LinkedIn or X posts
- threads and carousels
- short video scripts
- creator content planning
- launch or event posts
- community updates
- executive social posts
- content calendars

## Material Gate

Identify:

- audience, platform, brand voice, topic, and goal
- source facts, offer, announcement, or opinion
- desired CTA and risk constraints
- whether the content is personal, brand, executive, or community voice

Do not invent personal experiences, results, endorsements, or customer claims.

## Workflow

1. Define the audience and desired reaction.
2. Pick one post angle: insight, story, announcement, lesson, question, comparison, or CTA.
3. Draft a strong but accurate hook.
4. Keep structure platform-appropriate.
5. Provide variants by tone, length, or format when useful.
6. Flag claims that need evidence or approval.
7. Use `html-report-builder` for campaign calendars or social performance summaries.

## Checks

- Hook is clear without being misleading.
- Voice matches the account owner.
- Claims are fact-backed or softened.
- CTA fits the relationship with the audience.
- No internal-only information leaks into public copy.

## Outputs

- social post
- thread
- short-form script
- content calendar
- post variants
- launch/social announcement
- social content critique

## Boundary

This Skill owns social content framing, drafting, platform fit, and reuse planning. It does not own posting, scheduling, account access, analytics collection, paid boosting, influencer contracts, legal approval, or crisis response authority.

It must not run shell, Python, Node, npm, curl, MCP, social platform, scheduler, analytics, CMS, package install, publish, or external API commands by itself; those actions belong to XAgent tools visible in the current session.
