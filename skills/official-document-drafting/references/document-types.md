# Document Types

## Scope

This reference covers two groups:

- The 15 legal official-document types commonly used for Chinese Party and government documents.
- Common formal workplace materials used in offices, institutions, state-owned enterprises, and other organizations.

When unsure, first test whether the task is a legal official-document type. If not, choose the closest formal workplace material.

## Legal Official Documents

### 决议

Use for important decisions discussed and adopted by a meeting.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 会议背景
- 决议事项
- 贯彻执行要求
- 落款

### 决定

Use for important deployment, reward, punishment, handling, appointment, removal, or major action.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 背景和依据
- 决定事项
- 执行要求
- 落款

### 命令（令）

Use for publishing regulations, announcing major mandatory measures, or commending units and people by an authorized subject.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 发布依据
- 命令内容
- 生效时间或执行要求
- 签署与日期

Use cautiously; do not generalize it to ordinary work arrangements.

### 公报

Use for public release of important decisions, major matters, meeting outcomes, or statistical information.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 背景或会议概况
- 主要内容
- 结语

### 公告

Use to announce important or legally required matters domestically or internationally.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 公告事项
- 生效或执行说明
- 落款

### 通告

Use to publish matters that should be known or followed within a certain scope.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 发布缘由
- 通告事项
- 执行要求
- 落款

### 意见

Use to give views and handling methods for important issues.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 背景与总体要求
- 基本原则
- 主要意见
- 组织实施要求
- 落款

### 通知

Use to publish, transmit, forward, approve-forward, arrange, deploy, or appoint.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 主送单位
- 行文目的或背景
- 具体事项
- 工作要求
- 落款

Common ending: `特此通知。`

### 通报

Use to commend, criticize, or communicate important situations.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 基本情况
- 评价或问题分析
- 有关要求
- 落款

Common ending: `特此通报。`

### 报告

Use to report work, reflect situations, or answer inquiries upward.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 主送单位
- 基本情况
- 工作进展或主要情况
- 存在问题
- 下一步打算或建议
- 落款

Common ending: `特此报告。`

Do not include approval requests as the main purpose.

### 请示

Use to request instructions or approval from a higher-level authority.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 主送单位
- 事项背景
- 请示事项
- 拟办意见
- 结尾请批语
- 落款
- 附注（按用户模板或需要保留联系人）

Common endings:

- `妥否，请批示。`
- `以上请示，如无不妥，请予批准。`

Usually one document should cover one matter.

### 批复

Use to reply to a lower-level request.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 来文依据
- 批复意见
- 执行要求
- 落款

Common endings:

- `此复。`
- `特此批复。`

It must correspond to a concrete request.

### 议案

Use when governments submit matters for deliberation to the people's congress or its standing committee under legal procedure.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 提请审议事项
- 依据与理由
- 议案内容
- 落款

Do not use for ordinary internal approval requests.

### 函

Use for consultation, inquiry, request, reply, or approval matters between non-subordinate or parallel entities.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 主送单位
- 来函背景或事项缘由
- 主要意见
- 需对方配合事项
- 落款

Common endings:

- `特此函达。`
- `特此函复。`

### 纪要

Use to record main meeting situations and agreed matters.

Common structure:

- 会议基本信息
- 会议认为
- 议定事项
- 责任分工
- 后续要求

Do not write meeting minutes as a transcript. Only write consensus, decisions, and executable follow-up when supported by material.

## Common Formal Materials

### 工作方案 / 实施方案

Use for special actions, phased work, policy implementation, and project execution.

Common structure:

- 总体要求
- 工作目标
- 主要任务
- 实施步骤
- 保障措施

### 总结 / 工作总结

Use for staged review, annual summary, or special work summary.

Common structure:

- 基本情况
- 主要做法
- 工作成效
- 存在问题
- 下一步打算

### 简报 / 信息简报 / 新闻简报

Use for brief reporting of developments, meetings, stage results, and public news material.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 导语
- 主要信息
- 工作建议或提示

If current news or latest dynamics are involved, verify source and date or mark them pending verification.

### 情况专报 / 信息专报 / 舆情专报

Use for topical information, risk, public-opinion situation, incident progress, or matters reported to leaders or higher-level bodies.

Common structure:

- 事项概况
- 最新进展
- 风险研判
- 工作建议

Write facts before judgment, and mark source, date, and pending verification points.

### 讲话稿 / 发言稿

Use for leadership speech, meeting speech, mobilization, deployment, and summary comments.

Common structure:

- 开场说明
- 形势判断或背景引入
- 重点任务
- 工作要求
- 结束语

### 汇报材料

Use for reporting to leaders, inspection teams, or higher-level bodies.

Common structure:

- 工作进展
- 主要成效
- 存在问题
- 下一步措施

### 回复函

Use to formally reply to incoming letters, documents, consultations, or requested matters.

Common structure:

- 标题
- 主送单位
- 来文或事项依据
- 答复意见
- 办理说明或后续事项
- 落款

## Selection Checklist

- Need approval: `请示`.
- Reply to request: `批复`.
- Deploy work: `通知`.
- Principle-level methods: `意见`.
- Important handling or reward/punishment: `决定`.
- Public announcement: `公告` or `通告`.
- Upward status reporting: `报告`.
- Horizontal consultation or reply: `函`.
- Meeting consensus: `纪要`.
- News, current dynamics, risk, or topical information: `简报` or `专报`.
