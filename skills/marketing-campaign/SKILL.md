# Marketing Campaign

## Purpose

Create campaign plans and assets that connect audience, pain, offer, message, channel, CTA, timeline, and measurement.

## Use When

Use for:

- campaign briefs
- launch or demand generation campaigns
- lifecycle email campaign planning
- landing page messaging
- webinar or event promotion
- ad concept drafts
- campaign performance readouts

## Material Gate

Identify:

- target segment, persona, market, product, offer, and objective
- campaign stage, channel, budget or constraints, timeline, and CTA
- proof points, differentiators, objections, and compliance restrictions
- metrics such as leads, pipeline, conversion, activation, retention, or revenue

Do not invent performance benchmarks, customer proof, or compliance-approved claims.

## Workflow

1. Define objective and target audience.
2. Convert product features into audience problems and desired outcomes.
3. Choose campaign angle, offer, channel mix, and CTA.
4. Draft message hierarchy: headline, promise, proof, objection handling, CTA.
5. Create variants by persona, channel, or funnel stage when useful.
6. Define measurement and learning plan.
7. Use `html-report-builder` for campaign reports, calendars, or message matrices.

## Checks

- Audience and offer are specific.
- Main message states a clear reason to care.
- Proof points are real or marked missing.
- CTA fits funnel stage.
- Metrics connect to the campaign objective.
- Internal assumptions are not published as customer-facing claims.

## Outputs

- campaign brief
- message hierarchy
- landing page copy
- email campaign plan
- ad concept matrix
- channel calendar
- performance readout

## Boundary

This Skill owns marketing strategy structure, message planning, copy direction, campaign measurement, and asset review. It does not own ad spend, campaign sending, marketing automation configuration, tracking setup, legal approval, or brand approval.

It must not run shell, Python, Node, npm, curl, MCP, ad platform, marketing automation, email, CRM, CMS, analytics, package install, publish, or external API commands by itself; those actions belong to XAgent tools visible in the current session.
